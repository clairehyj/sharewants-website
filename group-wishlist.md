<section class="landing-section">
    <h2>The Problem with Group Gift-Giving</h2>
    <p>Group gift exchanges are supposed to be fun. But in practice, they often turn into logistical headaches. Someone creates a group chat to coordinate, people reply at different times, ideas get buried in the scroll, and someone inevitably buys the same thing as someone else. By the time the exchange actually happens, half the group is stressed and the other half gave up trying to find something good.</p>
    <p>The core issue is that group gift-giving requires coordination, and most of the tools people use for it, like text threads, emails, or shared documents, were not designed for this purpose. A dedicated group wishlist app like ShareWants solves these problems by giving every group member a structured, easy-to-browse list of things they actually want.</p>
    <p>With ShareWants, each person in the group creates their own wishlist by adding items from any online store. Everyone follows each other in the app, which means you can browse any group member's list whenever you need gift ideas. No more group chats, no more guessing, and no more accidentally buying the same sweater as three other people.</p>
</section>

<section class="landing-section">
    <h2>How Groups Use ShareWants</h2>
    <p>ShareWants works for any kind of group gift situation. Here are some of the most common ways groups use the app:</p>
    <p><strong>Friend Groups:</strong> Close friend groups often exchange gifts for birthdays and holidays. Instead of asking each friend what they want every time, everyone keeps a running wishlist on ShareWants. When a birthday comes up, you just open the app and pick something from their list. It is personal, it is easy, and it is always appreciated.</p>
    <p><strong>Office and Workplace Teams:</strong> Office gift exchanges, whether for the holidays or for work anniversaries, can be tricky. You might not know your coworker's taste well enough to pick something on your own. When everyone in the office has a ShareWants wishlist, you can browse their list and choose a gift within your budget that you know they will enjoy.</p>
    <p><strong>Clubs and Organizations:</strong> Book clubs, sports teams, hobby groups, and other organizations often have gift exchange events. ShareWants gives every member a way to share their preferences, making the process smooth and enjoyable for everyone involved.</p>
    <p><strong>Roommates:</strong> Roommates who want to exchange gifts for holidays or special occasions can use ShareWants to keep things simple. Each person maintains their own list, and everyone can shop for each other without the awkwardness of asking directly.</p>
    <p><strong>Extended Social Circles:</strong> When a large friend group or social circle wants to coordinate holiday gifts, the logistics can get out of hand. ShareWants scales effortlessly because there is no limit to how many people can follow each other. Whether the group has 6 people or 60, everyone gets a wishlist and everyone can see each other's lists.</p>
</section>

<section class="landing-section">
    <h2>Getting Your Group Started</h2>
    <p>Setting up ShareWants for your group is simple. Have everyone download the free app on iOS or Android. Each person creates their account and starts adding items they want to their wishlist. Then, group members follow each other using profile links or QR codes. Within a few minutes, the entire group is connected and ready for the next gift exchange.</p>
    <p>Encourage everyone to add a variety of items at different price points. This makes it easier for group members to find something that fits their budget. And because wishlists stay updated year-round, you will always have fresh ideas when the next occasion comes around.</p>
</section>
