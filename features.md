<div class="landing-section">
    <h2>How Adding Items Works</h2>
    <p>ShareWants gives you three easy ways to add items to your wishlist, so you can save gift ideas no matter where you find them.</p>

    <h3>1. Share Extension (Fastest)</h3>
    <p>While browsing any online store on your phone, tap the share button and select ShareWants. The app automatically extracts the product name, image, price, and link. It works with Safari, Chrome, Firefox, and any mobile browser.</p>

    <h3>2. AI Product Scanner</h3>
    <p>Found something you love in a physical store? Open ShareWants, tap the camera icon, and snap a photo. Our AI identifies the product, searches for it online, and adds it to your list with all the details filled in.</p>

    <h3>3. Paste a Link</h3>
    <p>Copy a product URL from anywhere and paste it directly into ShareWants. The app extracts all product information automatically from 24+ supported retailers.</p>
</div>

    <h3>Profile Sharing</h3>
