## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Active Noise Cancellation?
Active Noise Cancellation is a feature that reduces ambient sounds, allowing for a more immersive listening experience by using microphones to detect external sound and counteracting it with sound waves.

### Can I use AirPods Pro 3 with non-Apple devices?
Yes, the **AirPods Pro 3** can connect to any Bluetooth-enabled device, although some features may be limited when used with non-Apple products.
