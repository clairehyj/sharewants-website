<section class="landing-section">
    <h2>How to Create the Perfect Christmas Wishlist</h2>
    <p>Getting the most out of your holiday wish list starts with a few simple strategies:</p>
    <p><strong>Start early.</strong> Begin adding items to your Christmas wishlist as soon as you spot something you want — even months before the holidays. This gives gift givers more time to shop sales and find the best deals.</p>
    <p><strong>Include a range of prices.</strong> Mix affordable stocking stuffers with mid-range items and a few bigger-ticket gifts. This gives shoppers options no matter their budget, and ensures your list works for close family and casual acquaintances alike.</p>
    <p><strong>Be specific.</strong> Instead of "a new scarf," link to the exact scarf you want in the color and size you prefer. ShareWants pulls in product details automatically when you paste a URL, making specificity effortless.</p>
    <p><strong>Update regularly.</strong> If you buy something for yourself or change your mind, remove it from the list. Gift givers who follow you on ShareWants will always see the most current version.</p>
