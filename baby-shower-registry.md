    <h2>Why a Universal Baby Registry Makes More Sense</h2>
    <p>New parents need a wide range of baby essentials, and no single store carries everything. You might find the perfect stroller at one retailer, the safest car seat at another, and the cutest nursery decor at a small boutique shop. Traditional store-specific registries force you to pick one or two retailers and hope for the best. Guests end up juggling multiple registry links, and important items slip through the cracks.</p>
    <p>A universal baby shower registry on ShareWants eliminates this fragmentation. Add products from Amazon, Target, BuyBuy Baby, Pottery Barn Kids, Etsy, Walmart, and any other online store to a single, organized list. Your guests see one link, one page, and one clear picture of exactly what you need for your growing family.</p>
</section>

<section class="landing-section">
    <h2>How ShareWants Works for Baby Shower Registries</h2>
    <p>Setting up your baby registry on ShareWants is straightforward. Download the free app, create a new list, and start adding items. When you find a product on any website, copy its URL and paste it into ShareWants. The app automatically pulls in the product name, photo, and current price, so your registry looks professional without any manual data entry.</p>
    <p>Organize your baby shower registry by category if you like — nursery furniture, feeding supplies, clothing, diapering essentials, bath time, and travel gear. Add items at every price point so guests with different budgets can all find something meaningful to give.</p>
    <p>When your registry is ready, ShareWants generates a shareable link. Include it on your baby shower invitations, post it in family group chats, or add it to your personal page. Guests can browse and shop from any device — phone, tablet, or computer — without downloading the app themselves.</p>
    <p>As gifts are purchased, guests mark them off the list. This is visible to other shoppers so they know what has already been claimed, but it stays hidden from you. No duplicate presents, no awkward returns, and every gift is a genuine surprise.</p>

<section class="landing-section">
    <h2>What to Include on Your Baby Shower Registry</h2>
    <p>Building a baby registry for the first time can feel overwhelming. Here is a practical guide to the categories most new parents cover:</p>
    <p><strong>Nursery essentials.</strong> Crib or bassinet, crib mattress, fitted sheets, a baby monitor, blackout curtains, and a sound machine. These are often the higher-priced items on your list, and close family members usually appreciate having a meaningful gift to give.</p>
    <p><strong>Feeding supplies.</strong> Bottles, a breast pump, nursing pillow, bibs, burp cloths, a bottle warmer, and a high chair for later. If you plan to formula feed, adding your preferred formula brand helps guests contribute to everyday necessities.</p>
    <p><strong>Diapering.</strong> Diapers in multiple sizes, wipes, a diaper pail, changing pad, and diaper cream. These consumables are always welcome and make excellent gifts for guests who want to give something practical.</p>
    <p><strong>Clothing and swaddles.</strong> Onesies, sleepers, swaddle blankets, hats, socks, and a few outfits in larger sizes that your baby will grow into. Mix in items from boutique shops alongside budget-friendly basics.</p>
    <p><strong>Travel and gear.</strong> Car seat, stroller, diaper bag, baby carrier or wrap, and a portable changing pad. Research safety ratings ahead of time and link to the exact models you trust.</p>
    <p><strong>Bath time.</strong> Baby bathtub, hooded towels, washcloths, gentle soap, and a bath thermometer. These are affordable items that fill out your registry for budget-conscious guests.</p>
</section>
