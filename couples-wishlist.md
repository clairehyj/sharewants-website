<section class="landing-section">
    <h2>Why Every Couple Needs a Shared Wishlist</h2>
    <p>Giving gifts to the person you love should be exciting, not anxiety-inducing. Yet many couples fall into the same trap: asking "what do you want?" a week before the occasion, getting a vague answer like "I don't know, surprise me," and then spending hours trying to find something perfect. The result is often a gift that misses the mark, despite the best intentions.</p>
    <p>A couples wishlist changes the dynamic entirely. When both partners maintain running wishlists throughout the year, gift-giving becomes effortless. You spot a handbag you love while browsing Nordstrom? Save it to your ShareWants list in one tap. Your partner notices a new tool set on Amazon? They add it to theirs. When an occasion arrives, each of you has a curated collection of things the other person genuinely wants.</p>
    <p>The beauty of a wishlist for couples is that it preserves the element of surprise. Your partner knows you will pick something from their list, but they do not know which item. So every gift still feels thoughtful and exciting, because it is something they truly wanted, chosen by someone who cares about them.</p>
</section>

<section class="landing-section">
    <h2>Perfect for Every Occasion</h2>
    <p>A couples wishlist is not just for one holiday. Here are the moments when having a shared wishlist makes all the difference:</p>
    <p><strong>Anniversaries:</strong> Anniversary gifts carry extra weight because they celebrate your relationship. Having a wishlist ensures you pick something meaningful rather than resorting to a last-minute panic buy. Whether it is a piece of jewelry, a new piece of tech, or a cozy throw blanket, you will know it is something your partner has been wanting.</p>
    <p><strong>Birthdays:</strong> Birthdays come around every year, and after a few years together, it can get harder to think of new ideas. A running wishlist solves this by giving you a constantly refreshed list of options. Your partner adds items as they discover them, so there are always new ideas waiting for you.</p>
    <p><strong>Valentine's Day:</strong> Skip the generic chocolates and flowers (unless those are on their list). A wishlist lets you give something personal and specific, the kind of gift that shows you pay attention to what they love.</p>
    <p><strong>Christmas and Holidays:</strong> The holiday season is hectic enough without the added stress of gift shopping for your partner. With ShareWants, you can get their gift sorted early and focus on enjoying the season together.</p>
    <p><strong>Just Because:</strong> Some of the best gifts are unexpected. When you see your partner has added something small to their list, surprising them with it on a random Tuesday can mean more than any holiday present.</p>
</section>

<section class="landing-section">
    <h2>How to Get Started as a Couple</h2>
    <p>Getting set up is quick and simple. Both partners download ShareWants on their iPhone or Android device. Create your accounts, then follow each other using a profile link or QR code. Start adding items to your wishlists as you browse your favorite stores online. When you find something you want, use the share extension in your browser to save it directly to ShareWants.</p>
    <p>Over time, your wishlists grow naturally as you come across things you love. And when it is time to buy a gift, you already have a list of options that you know your partner will appreciate. No more stressful shopping trips or risky guesses.</p>
</section>
